Electricity Market Pricing

Novel stochastic programming formulations for market clearing with guaranteed cost recovery

Research Contributions

Cost recovery guarantee
Revenue adequacy
Stochastic formulations

Challenge

Traditional electricity market clearing mechanisms struggle with uncertainty from renewable energy sources, often failing to provide adequate cost recovery for generators while maintaining revenue adequacy for system operators. This creates financial instability and discourages investment in clean energy infrastructure.

Solution

Developed alternative stochastic programming formulations and pricing mechanisms that achieve:

  • Scenario-specific cost recovery for generators across all market scenarios
  • Revenue adequacy guarantees for system operators
  • Computational efficiency through advanced optimization techniques
  • Theoretical guarantees backed by rigorous mathematical analysis

Technical Implementation

Core Technologies:
C++ CPLEX Stochastic Programming Duality Theory Game Theory
Key Components:
  • Alternative Market Formulations: Multiple stochastic programming models with different objective functions and constraint structures
  • Nonanticipativity Constraints: Ensures decisions are made without knowledge of future uncertainty realizations
  • Dual-Based Pricing: Novel pricing mechanisms derived from optimization dual solutions
  • Computational Framework: High-performance C++ implementation with CPLEX optimization solver

Mathematical Innovation

The research introduces novel mathematical formulations addressing fundamental challenges in stochastic market design:

  • Scenario-Wise Cost Recovery: Ensures generator cost recovery in each individual scenario, not just in expectation
  • Revenue Adequacy: Guarantees sufficient revenue for system operators under all market conditions
  • Computational Tractability: Maintains polynomial-time solvability despite increased complexity
  • Theoretical Guarantees: Provides convergence and optimality proofs for the proposed algorithms

Results & Validation

Comprehensive testing on realistic power system test cases demonstrates the effectiveness of the approach:

  • Validated on IEEE power system test cases with renewable uncertainty
  • Improved computational efficiency compared to traditional methods
  • Achieved cost recovery across all tested scenarios
  • Published research available on arXiv with theoretical proofs

Future Applications

The methodology extends beyond electricity markets to other financial applications:

  • Portfolio Management: Risk-adjusted optimization with guaranteed returns
  • Commodity Trading: Pricing mechanisms under supply uncertainty
  • Financial Derivatives: Option pricing with scenario-specific guarantees
  • Supply Chain: Cost recovery in uncertain demand environments
