Study of Stochastic Market Clearing Problems in Power Systems with High Renewable Integration

Saumya Sakitha Sashrika Ariyarathne

Comprehensive doctoral research addressing three critical aspects of large-scale renewable energy integration into power systems: scenario generation for wind resources, innovative pricing mechanisms for stochastic electricity markets, and coordination systems for distributed grid operations.

Research Impact
Advanced wind pattern detection
Guaranteed cost recovery mechanisms
Distributed system coordination
Key Innovations:
  • Nonparametric Wind Modeling: Change point-based statistical simulation preserving spatio-temporal correlations and covariance structure
  • Advanced Market Design: Alternative stochastic programming formulations achieving scenario-wise cost recovery and revenue adequacy
  • Smart Grid Coordination: Nash equilibrium-based framework for multi-agent distribution system operations with minimal information sharing
  • Theoretical Foundation: Rigorous mathematical analysis with practical validation on real wind farm data and power system test cases
